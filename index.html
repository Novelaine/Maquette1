Life and technology change quickly. So does Ara.


The Ara frame contains all the functionnality of a smatphone plus six flexible slots for easy swapping


Ara modules fit neatly into the frame.
Allowing for upgraddes. Innovation and style.

Mix. Match. Swap.

Slide any Ara module into any slot and it just works. Powering this simplicity is Greybus, a nex bit of sofware deep in the Android stack. Greybus supports instantanaeus connections, power efficiency and data-transfer rates of up to 11.9 Gbps.

Built to Last

The Ara frame is built with durable latches and connectors to keep modules secured. Ara modules are designed around standards, allowing them to work with new generations of frames and new form factors.

Build with us

More space. new ideas

The Ara frame contains the GPU, antennas, sensors, battery ans display, freeing up more room for hardware in each module. We are looking to module makers to create technology never before seen on smatphones.

Hardware's API

The Ara baseplate provides on instant connection to the Ara frame and its core technologies. That means tess time fiddling and more time building.

Open marketplace

Were hard at work on an open marketplace to connect the next generation of ideas with the broader Ara community. Google is looking for beloved brands and forward-thinking developers to join us.
